The format

A casual competition built for bar night energy.

Bring your puzzle brain, sign up solo or with a team, and settle in for a table-vs-table challenge. Every team gets a different 500-piece puzzle at the same skill level. In regular play, each team's time is tracked cumulatively, and the first team to finish wins and keeps the puzzle.
